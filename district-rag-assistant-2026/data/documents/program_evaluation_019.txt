WICHITA PUBLIC SCHOOLS — ANNUAL PROGRAM EVALUATION SUMMARY
Document ID: PE-019
Program: ESOL Program
Academic Year: 2022-2023
Evaluator: Sabrina Fuller, Program Evaluation Office
Report Date: 2024-05-28

---
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The ESOL Program served 525 participants during the 2022-2023
academic year. Overall, the program met targets based on the district's
established performance benchmarks. This report summarizes program
activities, outcomes, budget utilization, and recommendations for the
upcoming academic year.

---
PROGRAM OVERVIEW
Description: ESOL Program is designed to provide targeted academic and
developmental support aligned with district strategic priorities.
Program Lead: James Mcconnell
Program Sites: 4 school locations across the district
Budget Allocated: $232,360
Budget Utilized: $211,294

---
PERFORMANCE OUTCOMES
Benchmark 1 — Participation Rate
  Target: 85% of enrolled participants complete the program.
  Actual: 89%
  Status: Met

Benchmark 2 — Academic Progress
  Target: 70% of participants demonstrate measurable academic growth.
  Actual: 87%
  Status: Met

Benchmark 3 — Stakeholder Satisfaction
  Target: 80% of surveyed stakeholders rate program as effective or better.
  Actual: 88%
  Status: Met

---
KEY FINDINGS
1. Staffing: Experienced mid-year turnover in two program sites.
2. Resources: Materials and technology were adequate.
3. Community Engagement: Family engagement events exceeded prior year attendance.

---
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR 2023-2024
1. Allocate supplemental budget for materials and resources.
2. Collect quarterly feedback from program participants.
