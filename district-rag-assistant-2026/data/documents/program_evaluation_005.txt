EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Early Childhood Education served 274 participants during the 2025-2026
academic year. Overall, the program partially met targets based on the district's
established performance benchmarks. This report summarizes program
activities, outcomes, budget utilization, and recommendations for the
upcoming academic year.
