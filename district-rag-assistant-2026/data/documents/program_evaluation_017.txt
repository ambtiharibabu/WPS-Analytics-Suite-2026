Program: Summer Bridge Program

Evaluator: Daniel Dawson, Program Evaluation Office

---
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Summer Bridge Program served 197 participants during the 2025-2026
academic year. Overall, the program met targets based on the district's
established performance benchmarks. This report summarizes program
activities, outcomes, budget utilization, and recommendations for the
upcoming academic year.

---
PROGRAM OVERVIEW
Description: Summer Bridge Program is designed to provide targeted academic and
developmental support aligned with district strategic priorities.
Program Lead: Eric Gardner
Program Sites: 2 school locations across the district
Budget Allocated: $121,536
Budget Utilized: $118,505

---
PERFORMANCE OUTCOMES
Benchmark 1 — Participation Rate
  Target: 85% of enrolled participants complete the program.
  Actual: 90%
  Status: Met

Benchmark 2 — Academic Progress
  Target: 70% of participants demonstrate measurable academic growth.
  Actual: 82%
  Status: Met

Benchmark 3 — Stakeholder Satisfaction
  Target: 80% of surveyed stakeholders rate program as effective or better.
  Actual: 87%
  Status: Not Met

---
KEY FINDINGS
1. Staffing: Recruitment challenges delayed program launch by three weeks.
2. Resources: Additional materials were procured mid-year from supplemental funds.
3. Community Engagement: New partnership with local nonprofit strengthened outreach efforts.

---
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR 2026-2027
1. Expand program enrollment by 15% next academic year.
2. Allocate supplemental budget for materials and resources.
