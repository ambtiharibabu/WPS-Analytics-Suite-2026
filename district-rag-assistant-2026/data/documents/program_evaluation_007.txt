Program: Special Education Support Services
Academic Year: 2024-2025
Evaluator: Kristi Wood, Program Evaluation Office
Report Date: 2025-06-28

---
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Special Education Support Services served 80 participants during the 2024-2025
academic year. Overall, the program partially met targets based on the district's
established performance benchmarks. This report summarizes program
activities, outcomes, budget utilization, and recommendations for the
upcoming academic year.

---
PROGRAM OVERVIEW
Description: Special Education Support Services is designed to provide targeted academic and
developmental support aligned with district strategic priorities.
Program Lead: Justin Chavez
Program Sites: 8 school locations across the district
Budget Allocated: $115,212
Budget Utilized: $103,897

---
PERFORMANCE OUTCOMES
Benchmark 1 — Participation Rate
  Target: 85% of enrolled participants complete the program.
  Actual: 92%
  Status: Not Met

Benchmark 2 — Academic Progress
  Target: 70% of participants demonstrate measurable academic growth.
  Actual: 86%
  Status: Met

Benchmark 3 — Stakeholder Satisfaction
  Target: 80% of surveyed stakeholders rate program as effective or better.
  Actual: 71%
  Status: Met

---
KEY FINDINGS
1. Staffing: Recruitment challenges delayed program launch by three weeks.
2. Resources: Materials and technology were adequate.
3. Community Engagement: New partnership with local nonprofit strengthened outreach efforts.

---
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR 2025-2026
1. Increase professional development hours for instructors.
2. Review assessment alignment with state standards.
