WICHITA PUBLIC SCHOOLS — ANNUAL PROGRAM EVALUATION SUMMARY
Document ID: PE-002
Program: Gifted & Talented
Academic Year: 2024-2025
Evaluator: Jennifer Lewis, Program Evaluation Office
Report Date: 2025-06-27

---
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Gifted & Talented served 238 participants during the 2024-2025
academic year. Overall, the program met targets based on the district's
established performance benchmarks. This report summarizes program
activities, outcomes, budget utilization, and recommendations for the
upcoming academic year.

---
PROGRAM OVERVIEW
Description: Gifted & Talented is designed to provide targeted academic and
developmental support aligned with district strategic priorities.
Program Lead: Alexandra Salinas
Program Sites: 5 school locations across the district
Budget Allocated: $82,598
Budget Utilized: $64,856

---
PERFORMANCE OUTCOMES
Benchmark 1 — Participation Rate
  Target: 85% of enrolled participants complete the program.
  Actual: 95%
  Status: Met

Benchmark 2 — Academic Progress
  Target: 70% of participants demonstrate measurable academic growth.
  Actual: 84%
  Status: Met

Benchmark 3 — Stakeholder Satisfaction
  Target: 80% of surveyed stakeholders rate program as effective or better.
  Actual: 92%
  Status: Met

---
KEY FINDINGS
1. Staffing: Fully staffed throughout the year.
2. Resources: Technology infrastructure needs upgrading at two sites.
3. Community Engagement: Family engagement events exceeded prior year attendance.

---
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR 2025-2026
1. Benchmark against peer districts using state data portal.
2. Increase professional development hours for instructors.
