WICHITA PUBLIC SCHOOLS — ANNUAL PROGRAM EVALUATION SUMMARY
Document ID: PE-004
Program: After-School Tutoring
Academic Year: 2025-2026
Evaluator: Terri Hall, Program Evaluation Office
Report Date: 2025-09-13

---
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The After-School Tutoring served 489 participants during the 2025-2026
academic year. Overall, the program partially met targets based on the district's
established performance benchmarks. This report summarizes program
activities, outcomes, budget utilization, and recommendations for the
upcoming academic year.

---
PROGRAM OVERVIEW
Description: After-School Tutoring is designed to provide targeted academic and
developmental support aligned with district strategic priorities.
Program Lead: Jeff Ortiz
Program Sites: 3 school locations across the district
Budget Allocated: $47,235
Budget Utilized: $42,602

---
PERFORMANCE OUTCOMES
Benchmark 1 — Participation Rate
  Target: 85% of enrolled participants complete the program.
  Actual: 80%
  Status: Not Met

Benchmark 2 — Academic Progress
  Target: 70% of participants demonstrate measurable academic growth.
  Actual: 85%
  Status: Not Met

Benchmark 3 — Stakeholder Satisfaction
  Target: 80% of surveyed stakeholders rate program as effective or better.
  Actual: 90%
  Status: Met

---
KEY FINDINGS
1. Staffing: Recruitment challenges delayed program launch by three weeks.
2. Resources: Additional materials were procured mid-year from supplemental funds.
3. Community Engagement: Family engagement events exceeded prior year attendance.

---
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR 2026-2027
1. Expand program enrollment by 15% next academic year.
2. Pilot additional support sessions for struggling participants.
