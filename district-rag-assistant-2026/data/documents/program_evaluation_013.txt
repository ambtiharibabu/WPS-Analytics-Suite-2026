WICHITA PUBLIC SCHOOLS — ANNUAL PROGRAM EVALUATION SUMMARY
Document ID: PE-013
Program: Summer Bridge Program
Academic Year: 2021-2022
Evaluator: Nathan Kline, Program Evaluation Office
Report Date: 2024-08-09

---
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Summer Bridge Program served 469 participants during the 2021-2022
academic year. Overall, the program did not meet targets based on the district's
established performance benchmarks. This report summarizes program
activities, outcomes, budget utilization, and recommendations for the
upcoming academic year.

---
PROGRAM OVERVIEW
Description: Summer Bridge Program is designed to provide targeted academic and
developmental support aligned with district strategic priorities.
Program Lead: Lisa Nelson
Program Sites: 8 school locations across the district
Budget Allocated: $41,545
Budget Utilized: $35,807

---
PERFORMANCE OUTCOMES
Benchmark 1 — Participation Rate
  Target: 85% of enrolled participants complete the program.
  Actual: 85%
  Status: Met

Benchmark 2 — Academic Progress
  Target: 70% of participants demonstrate measurable academic growth.
  Actual: 72%
  Status: Met

Benchmark 3 — Stakeholder Satisfaction
  Target: 80% of surveyed stakeholders rate program as effective or better.
  Actual: 73%
  Status: Met

---
KEY FINDINGS
1. Staffing: Fully staffed throughout the year.
2. Resources: Technology infrastructure needs upgrading at two sites.
3. Community Engagement: Attendance at community events remained below target.

---
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR 2022-2023
1. Pilot additional support sessions for struggling participants.
2. Benchmark against peer districts using state data portal.
