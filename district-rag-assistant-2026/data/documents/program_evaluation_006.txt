Program: Dual Language Program

Evaluator: James Phillips, Program Evaluation Office

---
EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The Dual Language Program served 599 participants during the 2023-2024
academic year. Overall, the program partially met targets based on the district's
established performance benchmarks. This report summarizes program
activities, outcomes, budget utilization, and recommendations for the
upcoming academic year.

---
PROGRAM OVERVIEW
Description: Dual Language Program is designed to provide targeted academic and
developmental support aligned with district strategic priorities.
Program Lead: Mary Williams
Program Sites: 5 school locations across the district
Budget Allocated: $214,821
Budget Utilized: $199,013

---
PERFORMANCE OUTCOMES
Benchmark 1 — Participation Rate
  Target: 85% of enrolled participants complete the program.
  Actual: 89%
  Status: Not Met

Benchmark 2 — Academic Progress
  Target: 70% of participants demonstrate measurable academic growth.
  Actual: 84%
  Status: Met

Benchmark 3 — Stakeholder Satisfaction
  Target: 80% of surveyed stakeholders rate program as effective or better.
  Actual: 77%
  Status: Met

---
KEY FINDINGS
1. Staffing: Fully staffed throughout the year.
2. Resources: Additional materials were procured mid-year from supplemental funds.
3. Community Engagement: New partnership with local nonprofit strengthened outreach efforts.

---
RECOMMENDATIONS FOR 2024-2025
1. Review assessment alignment with state standards.
2. Pilot additional support sessions for struggling participants.
